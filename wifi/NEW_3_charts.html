HOURLY AVERAGE TIME SPENT IN APPS

Chengliang Li

I have collected data for 10 days from Screen Time, which records the time I spend on different apps every hour in each day, in Settings
of my iPhone, and I calculated the average minutes I spent in my top 4 popular apps in every hour of the whole day. It’s easy to tell my
daily schedule (e.g. I usually slept from 3:00 – 10:00 during these 10 days) from all of three different ways of visualizations, and also
each of them has different advantages: From the scatter plot it’s evident to compare the different time spent per hour in each app; from
the bar chart the total minutes that I was using apps per hour are clear to contrast, as well as the Sankey diagram, from which the total
minutes spent in an app in the whole day is also obvious. From the visualizations it’s obvious that although the composition of percentage
of each hour is different, I spent the most time in WeChat (the main instant messaging app I use) almost in every hour, except for 19:00 –
20:00,in which I spent much more time in Alipay than other apps (I play a game in Alipay and from 19:00 to 19:30 there is the highest
chance to get scores), even though the total time for Alipay in a day is the least.

If you hover your mouse cursor over a dot, bar, node or link of these interactive charts, you will see a pop-up label showing how many
minutes I spent in an app or during a certain amount of time. Furthermore, you can drag the nodes in sankey diagram vertically to
make them in whatever order you want.
